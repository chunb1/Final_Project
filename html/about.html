Living in Portland has always been an amazing experience and really eye opening in terms of food and how different areas of the world differ and are similar in their cuisine. As a kid, I was also very interested in computers, technology, and math, so when I saw that coding was an available class at my school, I knew I had to take it, and I'm glad I did. When we had to brainstorm for ideas for our final website, I thought, what a great time for me to combine 2 things that I'm very passionate about, food and website development

Hello! My name is Sam. I am 16 years old and am just like you. I live in Portland, ME and go to Portland High School. I originally created this website with the idea of spreading knowledge about your own home town without having to explore every square inch of wherever it is that you live, and I thought what better way to do that than knowing about the restaurants in your area. Food n Stuff isn't a food delivery website, but rather a way to expand your taste and deepen your pallet. Some aspects of my website include the distance from your restaurant of choice, the cuisine of said restaurant, and some ratings.
